Chicken Katsu

Description

Easy Japanese-style fried chicken

Ingredients

  • Skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • Salt and pepper
  • Flour
  • Egg
  • Panko bread crumbs
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Season both sides of chicken breasts with salt and pepper.
  2. Coat chicken breasts in flour, dip into egg and press into panko crumbs until well coated on both sides.
  3. Place chicken in hot oil and fry until golden brown.
  4. Transfer to a paper towel-lined plate to drain.
  5. Ready to serve!

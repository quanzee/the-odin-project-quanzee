Crunchy French Onion Chicken

Description

Quicky and easy crunchy baked French onion chicken.

Ingredients

  • French-fried onions, crushed
  • 1 large eggr
  • Skinless, boneless chicken breast halves

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees.
  2. Spread crushed onions in a shallow bowl and pour egg into a separate bowl.
  3. Dip chicken into beaten egg then press into crushed onions.
  4. Place breaded chicken on a baking sheeet.
  5. Bake chicken until no longer pink in the center, about 20 minutes.
  6. Ready to serve!

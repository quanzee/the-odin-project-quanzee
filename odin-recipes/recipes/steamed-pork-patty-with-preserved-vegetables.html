Steamed Pork Patty With Preserved Vegetables

Description

A classic traditional Chinese recipe which is fast and easy to make.

Ingredients

  • Minced pork
  • Tianjin preserved vegetables
  • Shaoxing wine
  • Light soy sauce
  • White pepper
  • Sesame oil
  • Cornstarch
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Wash the preserved vegetables in water to wash off excess salt
  2. Chop the preserved vegetables finely and add to the minced pork in a mixing bowl.
  3. Add the sauces and seasoning to the mixing bowl.
  4. Whip everything together rapidly until the meat mixture becomes a smooth paste.
  5. Steam for 15 minutes.
